About Punjab Kings

Punjab Kings (formerly Kings XI Punjab) is a franchise cricket team based in Mohali, Punjab, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The team represents the state of Punjab and has developed a strong following since its inception in 2008. Punjab Kings is owned by Mohit Burman, Ness Wadia, and Preity Zinta, and they play their home matches at the IS Bindra Stadium in Mohali.
