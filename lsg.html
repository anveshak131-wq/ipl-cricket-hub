About the Team

Lucknow Super Giants is a franchise cricket team based in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The team was founded in 2021 alongside Gujarat Titans and made their debut in the 2022 season.

In their inaugural season, Lucknow Super Giants made an impressive debut by reaching the playoffs, finishing in the top four. The team is led by KL Rahul, one of India's most talented batsmen.

The team plays their home matches at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow. The Super Giants are owned by RPSG Group and have quickly become one of the most popular franchises in the IPL, known for their aggressive batting and strategic gameplay.
