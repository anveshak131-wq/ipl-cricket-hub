Title: How IPL Cricket Hub Works - A Complete Guide

[Opening Scene - 10 seconds]
Welcome to IPL Cricket Hub, your ultimate destination for Indian Premier League cricket information! This video will walk you through all the features of our modern, responsive website built with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

[Homepage Overview - 20 seconds]
Starting with the homepage, you'll see a beautiful grid of all 10 IPL teams with their official logos. Each team card has smooth hover animations and glassmorphism effects. Click on any team to open a modal with key information about that team.

The homepage also features animated statistics counters showing total matches, teams, and current season highlights. The navigation bar at the top allows easy access to all sections of the site.

[Navigation and Menu - 10 seconds]
The responsive navigation menu includes links to Fixtures, Points Table, Live Scores, About, and Contact pages. On mobile devices, it transforms into a hamburger menu for better usability.

[Fixtures Page - 20 seconds]
The Fixtures page displays the complete IPL match schedule in an interactive format. You can filter matches by team, match status (upcoming, live, completed), and date ranges. Each match card shows teams, venue, date, and time. Live matches are highlighted with special indicators.

[Points Table - 20 seconds]
The Points Table page shows the current standings with sortable columns for Position, Team, Played, Won, Lost, Tied, NRR, Points, and Recent Form. Click column headers to sort data. Team logos and form indicators make it easy to read at a glance.

[Live Scores - 15 seconds]
The Live Scores page provides real-time match updates with detailed scorecards, batting and bowling statistics, and live commentary. It shows current batsmen, bowlers, and match status.

[Team Pages - 15 seconds]
Each team has its own dedicated page with detailed information, squad list, and player profiles. Click on players to see their stats, roles (Captain, Vice-Captain, Wicket-Keeper), and additional details.


[Conclusion - 10 seconds]
IPL Cricket Hub is built with modern web technologies for fast loading and great user experience. Whether you're a die-hard fan or casual viewer, our site has everything you need to stay updated with IPL action!
